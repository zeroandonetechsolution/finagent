Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

FinAgent Pro provides digital financial management services and software. Since our services are purely digital, there is no physical shipping involved.

1. Delivery of Digital Services

Upon successful payment of your dues or registration fees, your digital passbook and account status will be updated in real-time within the application. You will receive immediate access to the updated financial records and confirmation of your transaction.

2. Service Activation

Access to chit fund groups or finance accounts is granted immediately after the administrator approves your request or verifies your initial payment. No physical documents are shipped as we operate on a completely paperless model.

3. Proof of Delivery

The digital transaction record in your account dashboard and the email/SMS confirmation (if enabled) serve as the proof of delivery for our services.

4. Queries

If you experience any delay in the update of your digital passbook after a successful payment, please contact our support team immediately.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

At FinAgent Pro, we aim to provide a seamless financial management experience. Please read our policy regarding cancellations and refunds carefully.

1. Cancellations

Membership or participation in a chit fund group can be cancelled subject to the group's specific bylaws. If you wish to cancel your participation, please contact the administrator. Cancellation may be subject to a processing fee or penalty as defined in the group agreement.

2. Refunds for Duplicate Payments

In case of any duplicate or accidental overpayment made through the application, the excess amount will be adjusted against your future dues by default. If a refund is specifically requested, it will be processed back to the original payment source within 7-10 working days after verification.

3. Non-Refundable Amounts

Subscription fees (if any), processing fees, and interest already paid are generally non-refundable once the service has been rendered or the period has passed.

4. Disputes

Any disputes regarding payments or account balances should be reported to us within 48 hours of the transaction. We will investigate and resolve the issue as per our internal records.

5. Contact for Refunds

For any refund-related queries, please email us at the contact address provided on our Contact Us page with your transaction ID and user details.
